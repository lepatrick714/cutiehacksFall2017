Coming Out Soon!

Welcome to the 4th Annual Cutie Hacks!


Cutie Hack is back this year for another round of hacking!

We are welcoming people of all skills levels to come out and join us for our 12 hour hackathon. Be prepared to juice out all the creativity you have!

Held again this year at the wonderful Winston Chung Hall at the University of California, Riverside on November 11th, 2017!

Frequently Asked Questions




Who can attend?

We currently only accept UCR and local community college students 18 or older to participate. High School students are welcome to apply, but will be required to sign a waiver.

What do I bring?

Bring your student ID, computer, and any peripherals you may need (i.e. chargers, monitors, etc). We encourage our hackers to bring any hardware that you plan on using.




Will there be food?

Yes! We will be providing meals along with refreshments and snacks throughout the event.

How much does Cutie Hack cost?

Cutie Hack is absolutely free to attend!




Do I need a team? How large can my team be?

We recommend working in a team. There will be a team formation event before hacking beings. Team sizes are capped at 4 members.

I signed up! When do I hear back?

Application results will be released 1 - 2 weeks pior to the event!
